QUAERENTEM'26

Organized by the Quiz Club of Mahamaya Girls’ College, Kandy, since 2004, QUAERENTEM is a premier annual all-island inter-school quiz competition. True to its tagline, “Questioning the Valor of Wisdom,” the event challenges students under the age of 19 to engage in critical thinking rather than simple factual recall. Aside from a temporary pause during the COVID-19 pandemic, this prestigious competition has run consistently, earning distinction as the first and only major quiz tournament in Kandy organized by a girls’ school. The competition is structured into Junior and Senior categories to ensure balance and accessibility for all participants. It begins with a written table round, transitions into a unique, themed special round that changes annually, and culminates in a high-pressure live oral round on stage for the top-performing teams. Held in the school auditorium, the rigorous event concludes with an official awarding ceremony celebrating the intellectual achievements of the contestants.

Our Story

About Us

Mahamaya Girls' College Quiz Club

Popularizing Quizzing
as a Mind Sport

Founded in 2004, the Quiz Club of Mahamaya Girls’ College, Kandy, was established with the aim of fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lasting passion for knowledge among students. Over the years, it has evolved from a small group of enthusiasts into one of Sri Lanka’s most formidable and recognized quizzing institutions, defined by consistency, discipline, and a strong competitive edge. In 2017, the club further expanded its reach with the launch of QUAERENTEM, its all-island inter-school quiz competition, attracting leading schools from across the island. Grounded in the belief that quizzing is not merely a competition but a discipline that sharpens the mind, the club continues to promote it as a legitimate and transformative intellectual pursuit, particularly empowering young women, while maintaining an outstanding record of achievements and accolades within the national quizzing circuit.

What We Stand For

MISSION
To enhance general knowledge and to popularize quizzing as a mind sport among Mayans.
VISION
To create a talented group of students with vast knowledge in different areas. We also hope to mould students who can shoulder our school towards the top of the Sri Lankan quizzing arena.
OBJECTIVE
To actively engage students in competitive quizzing, promote analytical thinking, and build confidence in academic and general knowledge competitions.

QUAERENTEM'26

Organized by Mahamaya Girls' College Quiz Club

Introduction

A Leading All-Island
Quiz Competition

QUAERENTEM — from the Latin quaerere, meaning "to seek" — is the flagship inter-school quiz competition organized by the Mahamaya Girls' College Quiz Club. Since its launch in 2017, QUAERENTEM has grown into a premier intellectual arena, bringing together the most competitive and knowledgeable student teams from schools across Sri Lanka.

Structure

Competition Format

Junior Quiz
A dedicated online competition for students under 15. Aspiring young minds from across the island compete virtually, with only the top 3 teams earning their place in the spotlight.
Stage 01
Table Quiz
The senior arena for U19 students, featuring five rigorous rounds of intellect: Round 1: Current Affairs Round 2: Science & Technology Round 3: Arts & Culture Round 4: History & Geography Round 5: Sports & Games
Stage 02
Special Round
Teams face a deep dive into specific, curated themes. These rounds test specialized knowledge beyond the general curriculum, with victors earning the prestigious title of Special Round Winners.
Stage 03
Grand Finale
The ultimate stage of the QUAERENTEM championship. The highest-scoring teams from the preceding rounds face off in a high-pressure finale to claim the championship title and trophies.
